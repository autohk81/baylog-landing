Features

Everything a Shop Manager Needs

No generic HR tools. No workarounds. BayLog was designed from the ground up for collision repair.

Pay Period Management
Open and close pay periods with one click. Lock submitted periods to keep payroll data clean.
RO-Based Hour Logging
Log hours directly against a Repair Order number. Body hours and paint hours tracked separately per technician.
PDF & Excel Export
Generate clean payroll reports as PDF or protected Excel files. Ready for accounting in seconds.
Multi-Shop Support
Managing more than one location? Owners and GMs see all shops. Managers stay scoped to their location.
Role-Based Access
Six roles built around how shops operate — from Owner and GM down to individual technicians.
Stat Holiday Tracking
Statutory holiday hours are separated in reports — so your accountant always has a complete picture.
