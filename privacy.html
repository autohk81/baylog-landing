Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

BayLog is operated out of British Columbia, Canada. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information in compliance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and BC's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA).

1. Who We Are

BayLog ("we", "us", "our") is a work-hour tracking application built for automotive collision repair shops. Our service is accessible at app.baylog.app. For privacy inquiries, contact us at support@baylog.app.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use BayLog:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and role within your shop.
  • Work data: Repair Order (RO) numbers, vehicle information, paint codes, and hours logged by technicians.
  • Company and shop information: Shop name, location, and pay period settings.
  • Usage data: Log-in activity and general app usage patterns.

We do not collect payment card information directly. Payments are handled by Stripe, which has its own privacy policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the BayLog application and its features.
  • Enable pay period management and payroll report generation.
  • Send account-related emails such as invitations, password resets, and notifications.
  • Respond to support requests sent to support@baylog.app.
  • Improve the application based on how it is used.

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

4. Where Your Data Is Stored

BayLog is built on the Base44 platform. Your data is stored on Base44's servers, which are located in the United States. By using BayLog, you consent to this cross-border transfer of data.

Base44 maintains industry-standard security practices including encryption and secure user management. For more details, see Base44's Privacy Policy.

5. Who Has Access to Your Data

Access to your data within BayLog is controlled by role-based permissions:

  • Technicians can only see their own work entries.
  • Shop Managers can see entries within their assigned shop.
  • Owners and General Managers have access across all shops under their company.
  • Accountants have read and export access across all shops.

BayLog staff may access data only when required to provide support, and only with your permission.

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, your data will be removed from our systems within 30 days, subject to any legal obligations to retain records.

7. Your Rights

Under PIPEDA and PIPA, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (which may affect your ability to use the service).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@baylog.app.

8. Cookies

BayLog uses session cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of BayLog after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
